My Thesis

CradleTalk: Advancing Parent-Infant Communication Through Real-Time Machine Learning Device Interpreting Dunstan Language

This project aims to develop a device called CRADLETALK that will help parents by detecting and understanding the sounds made by their babies in real-time. By using machine learning to interpret the Dunstan Language, the device enhances communication between parents and infants. This research project is designed to assist parents in meeting their babies' needs and improving the way they communicate with each other.
